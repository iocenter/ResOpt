void ResOpt::Well::setBhpLimit ( double  p) [inline]

Sets the BHP limit for the well.

The BHP limit is the minimum allowed pressure for producers, and the maximum allowes pressure for injectors. The limit is only used when the well is on rate control, and is not considered a constraint by the optimizer, only by the simulator.
